**Step 1 Sector Filter:** IF Defence selected THEN retrieve DASA Innovation Loan \+ NATO DIANA IF Healthcare selected THEN retrieve Biomedical Catalyst \+ NIHR IF Net Zero selected THEN retrieve Heat Pump Ready \+ Horizon Europe CleanTech IF Agri-tech selected THEN retrieve DEFRA ADOPT IF AI / Deep Tech selected THEN retrieve Smart Grants \+ EIC Accelerator

### **Calculation Formula**

**Intervention Rates (Innovate UK / Horizon Europe standards):**

* Micro / Small (1 to 49 employees): 70% intervention rate  
* Medium (50 to 249): 60% intervention rate  
* Large (250+): 50% intervention rate

**Conservative Estimate:** ProjectCost x InterventionRate x 0.80 **Optimistic Estimate:** ProjectCost x InterventionRate

**Display Format:** "Your estimated grant range: £\[Conservative\] to £\[Optimistic\]"

**Fundability Confidence Score:** Displayed as percentage (e.g., "78% Match") based on normalised total score. Framed as the gap to close not a perfect score by design.
